Introduction

Kobe Bryant, in full Kobe Bean Bryant, was an American professional basketball player. He was born on August 23, 1978 , in Philadelphia. He spent his entire 20-year career with Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Bryant led the NBA scoring twice, and ranks 4th in league all-time regular season and postseason scoring.(Wikipedia, 2023)

Interest in Basketball

Bryant started playing basketball at the age of three, and the Lakers were his favorite team when he was growing up. His father was also an NBA player. After moving to Reggio Emilia, he began to paly basketball seriously. His grandfather used to mail him videos of NBA games to study. At the age of 13, he and his family moved back to Philadelphia where he earned national recognition as basketbal player during spectacular high-school career at Lower Merion High School in Ardmore. This was the starting of Bryant's basketball career.

Professional Career

Kobe Bryant's illustrious 20-year NBA career with the Los Angeles Lakers, during which he formed iconic partnerships and achieved championship success, is marked by a remarkable legacy. He began as a promising rookie in 1996 and swiftly evolved into a scoring sensation. His partnership with Shaquille O'Neal led the Lakers to three consecutive NBA championships. Kobe's career spanned two decades with a single franchise, exemplifying loyalty and commitment rarely seen in professional sports. His retirement in 2016, symbolized by an emotional 60-point farewell game, remains an iconic moment in basketball history. Beyond his on-court success, Kobe's "Mamba Mentality" and dedication to excellence continue to inspire basketball enthusiasts and aspiring athletes, solidifying his status as a true basketball legend.

Achievements

The superstar played some 48,637 minutes in NBA, the sixth-most all time, according to the league statisticians. During his career, he totaled 33,643 points, includinng 5,640 in the Playoffs( both are the third best in the history of the NBA).

Some of Kobe Bryant's achievements include: six career 60-points games, 25 career 50-points games, 20 seasons with the Los Angeles Lakers(the most with one team in the NBA history ) and 18 consecutive All-Star Game selection is the league's 2nd longest streak, while only Kareem Abdul-Jabbar has more selections ever(19). Moreover, he was 5-times NBA Champion, 2-times Finals MVP and 1-time League MVP.

Last Game

Fan's final vision of Bryant in a purple and gold uniform seven and a half years ago was like many before as the Los Angeles Laker's legend scored a spectacular 60 points in his farewell NBA game. The Lakers defeated the Utah Jazz with the score of 101-96. Many notable stars were in attendance, including Snoop Dogg, Kanye West, Beyonce, Jay-Z, Magic Johnson and Shaquille O'Neal. In his final game, Bryant did the most Kobe Bryant thing ever, leaving everything he had on the hardwood of the Staples Center one last time. Outside the arena before the game against the Jazz on that day in April 13, 2016, fans had chanted, “Ko-be! Ko-be!”
